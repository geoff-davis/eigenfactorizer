The Eigenfactorizer Chrome Extension helps you find the best papers in PubMed. The extension color codes journal titles according to their Article Influence score. The score is computed in a manner similar to the way that Google's PageRank algorithm ranks web pages:

  • Journals whose articles are more frequently cited and cited in more influential journals receive a higher score.
  • Journals whose articles are less frequently cited and cited in less influential journals receive a lower score.
Color Journal's Article Influence Score
Highest Influence log(Score) is 2+ standard deviations above the mean
Higher Influence log(Score) is 1+ standard deviation above the mean
Above Average Influence log(Score) is above the mean
Below Average Influence log(Score) is below the mean
Lower Influence log(Score) is 1+ standard deviation below the mean
Lowest Influence log(Score) is 2+ standard deviations below the mean
Unknown Influence Unknown (not enough data available)
